Percentage of Kids in Alabama Counties

This choropleth reveals that there is a greater amount of children in the counties in the central to South Western portions of the state. There is a significant decline in the amount of children represented in the counties in the Northern and Eastern portion of the state.

African American Population, 2010

The spatial patterns displayed by this graduated symbol map show that there are the greatest amount of African Americans in states with major cities, and along the East coast.

Average Family Size and % of Black Individuals in AL Counties

This bivariate choropleth reveals that there is a strong correlation between these two variables in the lower portion of the state, with a weaker representation of both variables in the northern portion of the states.

Total Yearly Precipitation in Iowa

This isarithmic map was created by Professor Caglar Koylu. It displays a pattern in which there is a significantly greater amount of precipitation in North Eastern Iowa than there is in the rest of the state, with a gradual decline toward the South Western portion of the state.

Geovisual Analytics

These small multiples maps display trends of variables related to food access in Navajo County, Arizona. There is a correlation between areas with low access to supermarkets, low possession of vehicles, and low income level. This trend reveals that people of a lower income are at an increased risk of being unable to access food.
